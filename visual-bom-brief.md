| Visual | Role | Authority |
| --- | --- | --- |

## Authority Rule

Any image generated by an AI model or drawn as a concept view must be labeled
`concept_only` or `reference_only`. Fabrication authority must remain DXF, CAD,
design table, measured template, or reviewed drawing.
